About This App

This web app was created as a birthday gift for my girlfriend, who is a painter. I developed the app with the idea that it would be a useful tool to optimize her work process.

paletteHack includes a series of features designed for painters.

You can upload an image from your device and instantly get a palette of 10 colors. This feature will help you quickly assemble your color palette by identifying the predominant tones in the image. You'll also obtain the dominant color of the image, which corresponds to the main color that encompasses the entire image.

By clicking the "B&W" button, you can view a monochromatic version of your image, making it easy to identify grayscale values.

You also have the option to zoom in on the image so you don't miss a single detail.

When you click on any color in the obtained palette, you'll access a table with 5 luminance variations. These variations will be lighter if the original tone is dark, and vice versa. This function can be useful for determining the composition of certain colors, making the mixing process easier.
